Side Panel

Keep Learning Insights Within Reach

Unlike popups, the Chrome Side Panel stays open across tabs so the AI assistant can monitor activity nonstop.

Side Panel Features

  • Instant analytics view for time per topic, focus score, and comprehension index.
  • Live answer analysis so every response is evaluated in real time.
  • Topic focus tracking and smart suggestions for what to study next.
  • Quick access to settings, API validation, and device status.
  • Continuous monitoring without closing the panel, letting it feel like a persistent AI assistant.

Enable the Side Panel

  1. Click the Chrome extensions icon (🧩), find AI Learning Growth Tracker, and choose Open Side Panel.
  2. Optionally pin the extension via the Pin (📌) icon so you can open the panel quickly at any time.
  3. If Side Panel support is not visible, go to chrome://flags/, search for “Side Panel,” enable it, and restart Chrome.

Most modern Chrome builds already include Side Panel support.

Why the Side Panel Matters

  • It does not auto-close, so your session stays connected to AI insights.
  • It tracks activity across tabs and persists while you browse.
  • It supports continuous suggestions and data collection.
  • The panel feels like a dedicated AI study companion rather than a fleeting popup.
